# 20' Classic Gaff Sloop — Open Sail Plan (CC0

Summary
- Vessel: 20' LOA classic daysailer (Class J-inspired aesthetic).
- Rig: Gaff-headed sloop (single mast, gaff mainsail + jib on forestay).
- Spars: Carbon-fiber spars (mast, gaff, boom) — user-supplied / custom fittings.
- Sailcloth: Dacron (recommended: 6.6 oz/yd² cruising Dacron).
- Reefing: Two reef points on the mainsail (default), reef points in jib optional.
- Battens: 3 full battens in the mainsail (default).
- License: CC0 1.0 Universal (public domain dedication). See LICENSE.md.

What’s included
- SAIL_PLAN.md — full construction and sail plan, panel layouts, reinforcement, sewing specs, battens, reefing.
- BOM.md — bill of materials, cloth yardage estimate, hardware list, recommended sizes.
- MAINSAIL_refined_1to1_mm.svg — refined 1:1 SVG (mm) for mainsail (starter/master pattern).
- JIB_refined_1to1_mm.svg — refined 1:1 SVG (mm) for jib.
- MAINSAIL.dxf, JIB.dxf — ASCII DXF R12 polyline sail outlines.
- MAINSAIL_pattern.svg, JIB_pattern.svg — simplified starter SVG patterns.
- TILING_README_A4.md — printing & verification notes for A4 tiled prints.
- SPARS_AND_FITTINGS.md, TILING_INSTRUCTIONS.md — additional guidance.

Assumptions & defaults used
- LOA: 20 ft. Nominal beam: 6.5 ft. Sail area target: 300–330 ft² (mainsail ~220–230 ft², jib ~70–100 ft²).
- Mast/boom geometry are default values used in pattern generation; verify with your actual mast step and chainplate heights.
- Hull material: your choice — sail plan is hull-agnostic but sized to the dimensions above.
