# Carbon Spars & Fittings — Recommendations for 20' Gaff Sloop

This document gives recommended spar sizing guidance, common fitting types, and notes about terminations for carbon spars on the 20' gaff sloop.

Important safety note
- Carbon spars are high-performance components. Final tube OD, wall thickness, layup, and fittings must be selected with an experienced spar maker or rigger. The values below are starting recommendations for discussion with suppliers — not engineering guarantees.
